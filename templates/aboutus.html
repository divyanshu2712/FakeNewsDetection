Fake News Detector

Fake News Detector is a service to check whether the news given is real or fake. We at news corp has utilized the machine learning technique to develop the model to predict whether the news is fake or real. We have used Natural Language Processing Techinque , TF IDF Vectorization which is used to convert the text data set to vectors and then provide it to the trained model.The model we used in here for prediction has 94% accuracy which is quite good. We also offer this service to different company to deploy it to their applications or softwares. You can Contact Us if you want to deploy this service to your application.
